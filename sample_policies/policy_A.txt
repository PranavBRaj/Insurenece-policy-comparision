HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: BlueShield Premier Health Plan
Policy Number: BSP-2024-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Insurer: BlueShield Insurance Co.

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $500,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $200,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners and specialist doctors.
Coverage limit: Up to 30 outpatient visits per year at $150 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ambulance services.
Emergency room: $5,000 per incident.
Ambulance: $1,000 per incident.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary surgeries including pre-operative and post-operative care.
Coverage up to $150,000 per surgical event.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic and branded drugs listed in the approved formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $5,000.
Generic drugs: 90% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: 70% reimbursement.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers inpatient and outpatient mental health treatment.
Up to 20 outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient mental health: up to 30 days per year.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Covers prenatal visits, delivery, and postnatal care.
Normal delivery: $8,000.
Caesarean section: $12,000.
Newborn care up to 30 days post-birth.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual wellness check-up: 1 per year, fully covered (100%).
Vaccinations as recommended by WHO schedule: fully covered.
Cancer screening (mammogram, Pap smear, PSA test): covered once per year.

1.9 Dental Care (Rider)
Basic dental: fillings, extractions, scaling.
Annual dental limit: $1,000.
Orthodontics not covered under this plan.

1.10 Vision Care (Rider)
Annual eye examination: covered once per year.
Corrective lenses or frames: $300 per year.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Any illness or condition diagnosed or treated within 12 months before the
policy start date is not covered during the first 24 months.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Any surgery or treatment primarily for aesthetic purposes is excluded,
including rhinoplasty, liposuction, and non-reconstructive breast surgery.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Treatments not approved by the relevant health authority or classified as
investigational or experimental are not covered.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
Injuries or conditions resulting from intentional self-harm, attempted
suicide, or participation in self-destructive activities are excluded.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Treatment for alcohol or drug dependency is not covered unless admitted
through our network rehabilitation program under special approval.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Injuries or illnesses arising directly from war, invasion, or civil
disturbance are excluded from all coverage.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
In vitro fertilization (IVF), artificial insemination, and other assisted
reproductive technologies are not covered.

2.8 Alternative Medicine
Homeopathy, acupuncture, naturopathy, and other alternative medicine
treatments are excluded unless prescribed by a licensed physician.

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $2,400.00
Monthly Premium: $200.00
Deductible: $500.00 per year
Co-payment: $25.00 per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 20% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $4,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:
- Policy administration fee: $50.00 per year
- Dental rider premium: $15.00 per month
- Vision rider premium: $10.00 per month

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy provides full benefits when using in-network providers.
Out-of-network services are reimbursed at 60% of the approved amount.
Emergency services are covered at in-network rates regardless of provider.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 90 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is required for all elective surgeries and specialist referrals.
Reimbursement is processed within 15 business days of claim approval.
