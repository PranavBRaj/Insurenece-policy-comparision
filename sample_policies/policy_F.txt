HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: FamilyShield Choice Health Plan
Policy Number: FSC-2026-005
Effective Date: September 1, 2026
Insurer: FamilyShield Insurance Partners

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $500,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $150,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners, pediatricians, and specialists.
Coverage limit: Up to 40 outpatient visits per year at $30 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ambulance services.
Emergency room: $5,000 per incident.
Ambulance: $800 per incident.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary surgeries including pre-operative and post-operative care.
Coverage up to $100,000 per surgical event.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic, branded, and pediatric specialty drugs listed in the approved formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $7,500.
Generic drugs: 90% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: 60% reimbursement.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers inpatient and outpatient mental health treatment, including family counseling.
Up to 25 outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient mental health: up to 20 days per year.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Covers prenatal visits, delivery, and postnatal care.
Normal delivery: $10,000.
Caesarean section: $15,000.
Newborn care up to 45 days post-birth, including NICU up to $50,000.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual wellness check-up: 1 per year, fully covered (100%).
Vaccinations as recommended by WHO schedule, including full pediatric schedule: fully covered.
Cancer screening: covered once every year for adults over 40.

1.9 Dental Care (Included for Dependents Under 18)
Basic dental for minors: fillings, extractions, scaling, sealants.
Adult Dental Care (Rider): Annual limit $1,500.
Orthodontics: Covered at 50% for minors only, up to $2,500 lifetime.

1.10 Vision Care (Included for Dependents Under 18)
Eye examination: covered once per year.
Corrective lenses or frames for minors: $200 per year.
Adult Vision Care (Rider): $250 per year for frames/lenses.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Any illness or condition diagnosed or treated within 12 months before the
policy start date is not covered during the first 12 months.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Any surgery or treatment primarily for aesthetic purposes is excluded,
except for reconstructive surgery required due to congenital defects in covered newborns.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Treatments not approved by the relevant health authority or classified as
investigational or experimental are not covered.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
Covered only for initial emergency stabilization up to $5,000.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Treatment for alcohol or drug dependency is covered for up to 15 days of
inpatient rehabilitation and 10 outpatient sessions per year.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Injuries or illnesses arising directly from war, invasion, or civil
disturbance are excluded from all coverage.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
Diagnostics covered up to $1,000. In vitro fertilization (IVF) and artificial
insemination are excluded.

2.8 Alternative Medicine
Acupuncture and chiropractic care are covered up to a combined limit of
$500 per year. All other alternative therapies are excluded.

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $3,600.00 (Family Plan - Up to 4 members)
Monthly Premium: $300.00
Deductible: $1,500.00 per year (Family Aggregate)
Co-payment: $30.00 per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 20% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $8,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:

* Policy administration fee: $75.00 per year
* Adult Dental rider premium: $25.00 per month
* Adult Vision rider premium: $15.00 per month

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy is a Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) plan.
This policy provides maximum benefits when using in-network providers.
Out-of-network services are reimbursed at 70% of the customary and reasonable amount.
Emergency pediatric services are covered at in-network rates globally.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 120 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is required for all elective surgeries, advanced imaging (MRI/CT), and NICU admissions.
Reimbursement is processed within 20 business days of claim approval.
