HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: Horizon Global Nomad Health Plan
Policy Number: HGN-2027-007
Effective Date: January 1, 2027
Insurer: Horizon International Medical

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays globally, including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $1,500,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $300,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners and specialists worldwide.
Coverage limit: Up to 20 outpatient visits per year.
Telehealth consultations: Unlimited and fully covered (100%).

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ambulance services globally.
Emergency room: $0 copay if admitted, $250 if not admitted.
Medical Evacuation & Repatriation: Fully covered up to $500,000 per incident.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary surgeries including pre-operative and post-operative care.
Coverage up to $200,000 per surgical event.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic and branded drugs prescribed by a licensed physician globally.
Annual drug benefit limit: $4,000.
Generic drugs: 100% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: 70% reimbursement.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers inpatient and outpatient mental health treatment.
Up to 15 virtual or in-person outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient mental health: up to 20 days per year.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Subject to a 10-month waiting period from the effective date.
Normal delivery: $8,000.
Caesarean section: $12,000.
Newborn care up to 30 days post-birth.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual global wellness check-up: 1 per year, fully covered (100%).
Vaccinations, including travel vaccines (e.g., Yellow Fever, Typhoid): fully covered.
Cancer screening: covered once per year.

1.9 Dental Care (Adventure & Dental Rider)
Basic dental: fillings, extractions, scaling.
Annual dental limit: $800.
Orthodontics not covered.

1.10 Vision Care (Adventure & Dental Rider)
Annual eye examination: covered once per year.
Corrective lenses or frames: $200 per year.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Any illness or condition diagnosed or treated within 12 months before the
policy start date is not covered during the first 12 months, unless transferring
continuous coverage from another recognized international health plan.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Any surgery or treatment primarily for aesthetic purposes is excluded.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Treatments not approved by the WHO or relevant national health authority are excluded.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
Injuries or conditions resulting from intentional self-harm or attempted
suicide are excluded.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Treatment for alcohol or drug dependency is not covered.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Covered for passive acts of terrorism. Excluded if the insured travels to a country
where a Level 4 "Do Not Travel" advisory is in effect prior to their arrival.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
In vitro fertilization (IVF), artificial insemination, and other assisted
reproductive technologies are entirely excluded.

2.8 Extreme Sports
Injuries from hazardous activities (e.g., scuba diving below 30m, skydiving,
mountaineering with ropes) are excluded unless the Adventure Rider is purchased.

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $4,200.00
Monthly Premium: $350.00
Deductible: $250.00 per year
Co-payment: $0.00 per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 10% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $3,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:

* Policy administration fee: $0.00
* Adventure & Dental Rider premium: $45.00 per month

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy operates on an open-access global network.
The insured may visit any licensed medical practitioner or facility worldwide.
Direct billing is available within the Horizon Global Provider Network.
Out-of-network claims (outside the direct billing network) are reimbursed at 90%
of the customary and reasonable amount for that specific country.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted via the global portal within 180 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is strictly required for medical evacuation and non-emergency inpatient admissions.
Reimbursement is processed in the policyholder's chosen currency within 10 business days.
