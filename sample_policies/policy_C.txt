HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: GreenCross Standard Health Plan
Policy Number: GCS-2024-002
Effective Date: March 1, 2024
Insurer: GreenCross Insurance Ltd.

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $250,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $100,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners and specialist doctors.
Coverage limit: Up to 20 outpatient visits per year at $100 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ambulance services.
Emergency room: $3,000 per incident.
Ambulance: $500 per incident.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary surgeries including pre-operative and post-operative care.
Coverage up to $75,000 per surgical event.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic and branded drugs listed in the approved formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $3,000.
Generic drugs: 80% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: 50% reimbursement.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers inpatient and outpatient mental health treatment.
Up to 10 outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient mental health: up to 15 days per year.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Covers prenatal visits, delivery, and postnatal care.
Normal delivery: $5,000.
Caesarean section: $8,000.
Newborn care up to 15 days post-birth.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual wellness check-up: 1 per year, fully covered (100%).
Vaccinations as recommended by WHO schedule: 80% covered.
Cancer screening (mammogram, Pap smear, PSA test): covered once every 2 years.

1.9 Dental Care (Rider)
Basic dental: fillings, extractions, scaling.
Annual dental limit: $500.
Orthodontics: 50% coverage up to a lifetime maximum of $2,000.

1.10 Vision Care (Rider)
Eye examination: covered once every 2 years.
Corrective lenses or frames: $150 per year.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Any illness or condition diagnosed or treated within 6 months before the
policy start date is not covered during the first 12 months.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Any surgery or treatment primarily for aesthetic purposes is excluded,
unless required for reconstructive purposes following a covered accident.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Treatments not approved by the relevant health authority or classified as
investigational or experimental are strictly excluded.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
Injuries or conditions resulting from intentional self-harm or attempted
suicide are excluded.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Treatment for alcohol or drug dependency is covered up to 30 days of inpatient
rehabilitation once per lifetime.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Injuries or illnesses arising directly from war, invasion, or civil
disturbance are excluded from all coverage.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
In vitro fertilization (IVF) and artificial insemination are covered
up to a lifetime limit of $5,000.

2.8 Alternative Medicine
Homeopathy and naturopathy are excluded. Acupuncture is covered up to
10 sessions per year when prescribed for pain management.

---

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $1,200.00
Monthly Premium: $100.00
Deductible: $1,000.00 per year
Co-payment: $35.00 per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 30% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $6,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:

* Policy administration fee: $25.00 per year
* Dental rider premium: $10.00 per month
* Vision rider premium: $5.00 per month

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy provides maximum benefits when using in-network providers.
Out-of-network services are reimbursed at 50% of the approved amount.
Emergency services are covered at in-network rates regardless of provider facility.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 60 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is required for all inpatient admissions and surgeries.
Reimbursement is processed within 30 business days of claim approval.
