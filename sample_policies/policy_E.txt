HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: ValueCare Essential Health Plan
Policy Number: VCE-2026-004
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Insurer: ValueCare Mutual

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $50,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $15,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners only. Specialist visits require referrals.
Coverage limit: Up to 5 outpatient visits per year at $50 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits for life-threatening conditions only.
Emergency room: $1,500 per incident.
Ambulance: Not covered under this plan.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers emergency surgeries and strictly medically necessary procedures.
Coverage up to $25,000 per surgical event.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic drugs only, as listed in the basic formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $500.
Generic drugs: 50% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: Not covered.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers emergency psychiatric evaluation only.
Inpatient mental health: up to 5 days per lifetime.
Outpatient therapy sessions: Not covered.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Maternity coverage is limited to complications of pregnancy.
Routine prenatal visits and normal delivery: Not covered.
Newborn care: Covered for the first 48 hours post-birth.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual wellness check-up: 1 per year, covered at 50%.
Vaccinations as recommended by WHO schedule: covered at 50%.
Cancer screening: Not covered under this basic plan.

1.9 Dental Care
Not included. Dental riders are not available for this plan tier.

1.10 Vision Care
Not included. Vision riders are not available for this plan tier.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Any illness or condition diagnosed, treated, or showing symptoms within 24 months
before the policy start date is permanently excluded from coverage.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Strictly excluded, including all reconstructive surgeries regardless of cause.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Strictly excluded.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
All injuries or conditions resulting from intentional self-harm are strictly excluded.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Treatment for alcohol or drug dependency is not covered.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Excluded from all coverage.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
Excluded from all coverage.

2.8 Alternative Medicine
All forms of alternative, complementary, and holistic medicine are excluded.

2.9 Sports and Extreme Activities
Injuries sustained during organized sports, extreme sports, or hazardous activities
(e.g., rock climbing, scuba diving) are excluded.

---

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $600.00
Monthly Premium: $50.00
Deductible: $5,000.00 per year
Co-payment: $50.00 per general practitioner visit
Co-insurance: 50% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $15,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:

* Policy administration fee: $100.00 per year

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy is a strict Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) plan.
Services must be rendered by in-network providers.
Out-of-network services are not covered (0% reimbursement) except for true medical emergencies.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is strictly required for any service exceeding $500.
Reimbursement is processed within 45 business days of claim approval.
