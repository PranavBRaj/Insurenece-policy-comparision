HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: MediCare Gold Plus Plan
Policy Number: MGP-2024-088
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Insurer: MediCare Insurance Group

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room, board, and nursing care.
Coverage limit: $300,000 per policy year.
ICU/Critical Care: covered up to $100,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners and specialists.
Coverage limit: Up to 20 outpatient visits per year at $100 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ground ambulance.
Emergency room: $3,000 per incident.
Ambulance (ground only): $800 per incident.
Air ambulance: NOT covered.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary surgeries.
Coverage up to $100,000 per surgical event.
Robotic surgery requires prior approval and incurs 30% additional co-pay.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers only generic drugs listed in the approved formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $3,000.
Generic drugs: 80% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: NOT covered unless no generic equivalent exists.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers outpatient mental health services only.
Up to 12 outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient psychiatric care: NOT covered.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Covers prenatal visits and delivery.
Normal delivery: $6,000.
Caesarean section: $9,000.
Newborn care limited to 7 days post-birth.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual wellness check-up: 1 per year, fully covered (100%).
Core vaccinations: fully covered.
Comprehensive cancer screening package: covered once every two years.

1.9 Chronic Disease Management
Covers ongoing monitoring and treatment for diabetes, hypertension,
and asthma including regular physician visits and approved medications.
Annual limit: $2,500 per chronic condition.

1.10 Physiotherapy
Covers physiotherapy sessions prescribed by a licensed physician.
Up to 15 sessions per year at $80 per session.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Any illness or condition diagnosed or treated within 24 months before the
policy effective date is excluded for the first 36 months.

2.2 Cosmetic and Elective Procedures
Any surgery, treatment, or procedure primarily for cosmetic or elective
purposes is excluded unless deemed medically necessary by two independent
physicians.

2.3 Experimental and Unproven Treatments
Treatments classified as experimental, investigational, or not supported
by evidence-based medical guidelines are not covered.

2.4 Self-inflicted Harm
Injuries or illnesses resulting from intentional self-harm, suicide
attempts, or deliberate risk-taking activities are excluded.

2.5 Substance Abuse and Addiction
Treatment for alcohol dependency, drug addiction, or substance abuse
disorders is not covered under this plan.

2.6 Dental and Vision Care
Routine dental and vision care is not included in this plan.
Emergency dental treatment resulting from an accident is covered up to $500.

2.7 Obesity Treatment
Weight loss surgery, treatment for obesity, and related complications
are excluded unless caused by a documented endocrine disorder.

2.8 Travel Vaccinations
Vaccinations required for international travel purposes are not covered.

2.9 War, Terrorism, and Nuclear Events
Any injury or illness arising from acts of war, terrorism, nuclear radiation,
or chemical/biological attacks is excluded.

---

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $1,800.00
Monthly Premium: $150.00
Deductible: $1,000.00 per year
Co-payment: $30.00 per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 30% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $6,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:
- Policy administration fee: $40.00 per year
- Late payment fee: $20.00 per month if premium is overdue by 15+ days

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

Full benefits apply at MediCare Gold network hospitals and clinics.
Out-of-network services are reimbursed at 50% of the approved amount.
Emergency services at non-network facilities covered at 70% of approved rates.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 60 days of the date of service.
All elective surgeries and specialist referrals require pre-authorization
at least 5 business days in advance.
Reimbursement processed within 20 business days of claim approval.
