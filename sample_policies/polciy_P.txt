HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

Policy Name: Infernal Soul Preservation & Torment Mitigation Plan
Policy Number: STYX-666-BRIMSTONE
Effective Date: The Moment of Your Demise
Insurer: The Ninth Circle Mutual & Underworld Underwriters

**SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS**

**1.1 Purgatorial Waiting Room Care**
Covers infinite residency in the Gray Wastes while your final destination is processed.
Includes unlimited access to lukewarm tap water, a single folding chair with one short leg, and magazines exclusively from the year 2004.
Sub-limit: Dental coverage for teeth grinding while waiting covered up to 50 eternities.

**1.2 Hellfire & Brimstone Burn Management**
Covers superficial to soul-deep spiritual burns sustained in the lower circles.
Includes daily application of mildly soothing, yet ultimately ineffective, sulfur-based aloe vera.
Coverage limit: Up to 3,000,000 Screams of Agony per billing cycle.

**1.3 Reincarnation Roulette (Premium Tier Only)**
If eligible for mortal rebirth, this policy provides one (1) mandatory reroll if your assigned next life is a dung beetle, a mayfly, or a medieval peasant.
Karma point deduction: 15,000 points per reroll.

**1.4 Poltergeist & Haunting Liability**
Covers ectoplasmic damage, broken dishware, and structural chilling costs if you are approved for a temporary haunting visa to terrorize your living descendants.
Banshee wailing throat lozenges: Fully covered (100%).

**1.5 Demonic Possession Insurance**
If you successfully possess a mortal vessel, this covers routine maintenance, exorcism evasion tactics, and repairs if you accidentally total the host body by doing too much unnatural back-bending.

**SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS**

**2.1 Blood Pacts & Pre-existing Crossroads Deals**
Any soul forfeited to a crossroads demon, archfiend, or minor imp prior to your clinical death is not eligible for preservation. The collection agency has first rights.

**2.2 The Sisyphus Clause**
Physical therapy, joint replacement, or chiropractic adjustments for repetitive strain injuries caused by eternally pushing a boulder up a hill are strictly excluded.

**2.3 Divine Intervention & Rapture**
If you are accidentally beamed up into a heavenly host, absolved of your sins, or rescued by an angelic strike team, this policy is immediately voided and no premium refunds will be issued.

**2.4 Irony-Based Punishments**
Any psychological trauma resulting from tailor-made, ironically fitting eternal punishments (e.g., a glutton being forced to eat plastic fruit forever) is considered standard operating procedure and is not covered.

**2.5 Unsanctioned Necromancy**
If a mortal wizard yanks your soul back into a decaying corpse without submitting the proper Reanimation Requisition Forms, you are on your own.

**SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS**

Annual Premium: Three (3) corrupted virtues and the memory of your first love.
Monthly Premium: A continuous, low-frequency hum of existential despair.
Deductible: 500 consecutive years treading water in the Lake of Fire.
Co-payment: Surrendering one fond childhood memory per visit to the Torment Clinic.
Co-insurance: 10% of your remaining willpower after the deductible is met.
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: The complete and total loss of your individual identity.
Additional Fees:
Ferryman transit fee (Charon’s Toll): 2 Gold Coins (MUST be placed on eyes prior to burial).

**SECTION 4: NETWORK**

This policy operates on a subterranean, multi-tiered network of suffering.
The insured may seek care in Hades, Tartarus, Gehenna, or the DMV.
Direct billing is available within the Archdemon Provider Network.
Out-of-network claims (attempting to seek shelter in Valhalla, Elysium, or The Good Place) are considered acts of cosmic treason and will result in your immediate demotion to the sub-basement of the abyss.

**SECTION 5: CLAIMS**

Claims must be submitted via Ouija board, seance, or by whispering them backward into a blood-stained mirror at exactly 3:33 AM.
Pre-authorization is strictly required before shedding your astral form or attempting to unionize the damned.
Reimbursement is processed in the currency of pure malice, and processing times may vary between ten thousand years and the eventual collapse of the multiverse.
