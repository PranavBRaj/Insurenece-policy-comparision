HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: CoreGuard Catastrophic Health Plan
Policy Number: CGC-2026-006
Effective Date: November 1, 2026
Insurer: CoreGuard Assurance

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $2,000,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $500,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Not covered until the annual deductible is met.
Coverage limit post-deductible: Up to 3 outpatient visits per year at $40 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ambulance services for life-threatening events.
Emergency room: $1,000 copay after deductible is met.
Ambulance: Fully covered (100%) after deductible is met.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary emergency and inpatient surgeries.
Coverage up to $250,000 per surgical event after deductible is met.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic and branded drugs listed in the approved formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $10,000.
Generic drugs: 100% reimbursement after deductible.
Branded drugs: 80% reimbursement after deductible.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers acute inpatient mental health treatment only.
Inpatient mental health: up to 14 days per year.
Outpatient therapy sessions: Not covered.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Maternity coverage is strictly limited to life-threatening complications of pregnancy.
Routine prenatal visits, normal delivery, and elective Caesarean sections: Not covered.
Newborn emergency care up to 7 days post-birth.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual wellness check-up: 1 per year, fully covered (100%) regardless of deductible.
Vaccinations as recommended by WHO schedule: fully covered.
Cancer screening (mammogram, Pap smear, PSA test): covered once per year.

1.9 Dental Care
Not included. Dental riders are not available for this plan.

1.10 Vision Care
Not included. Vision riders are not available for this plan.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Any illness or condition diagnosed or treated within 36 months before the
policy start date is not covered during the first 24 months.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Any surgery or treatment primarily for aesthetic purposes is strictly excluded.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Treatments not approved by the relevant health authority or classified as
investigational or experimental are not covered.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
Injuries or conditions resulting from intentional self-harm, attempted
suicide, or participation in self-destructive activities are excluded.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Treatment for alcohol or drug dependency, including rehabilitation, is completely excluded.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Injuries or illnesses arising directly from war, invasion, or civil
disturbance are excluded from all coverage.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
In vitro fertilization (IVF), artificial insemination, and diagnostics are not covered.

2.8 Alternative Medicine
Homeopathy, acupuncture, naturopathy, chiropractic care, and other alternative
medicine treatments are excluded.

---

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $900.00
Monthly Premium: $75.00
Deductible: $9,500.00 per year
Co-payment: $40.00 per outpatient visit (post-deductible)
Co-insurance: 20% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $12,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:

* Policy administration fee: $20.00 per year

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy is an Exclusive Provider Organization (EPO) plan.
This policy provides benefits ONLY when using in-network providers.
Out-of-network services are entirely excluded (0% reimbursement) except for true,
life-threatening medical emergencies admitted through an ER.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 90 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is strictly required for any hospital admission.
Reimbursement is processed within 15 business days of claim approval.
