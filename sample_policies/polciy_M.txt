HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

Policy Name: Omniversal Nonsense & Cartoon Physics Assurance Plan
Policy Number: 8675309-BANANA-FLAPJACK
Effective Date: Yesterday, Tomorrow, and the color Bleen
Insurer: The Grand High Council of Three Raccoons in a Trenchcoat

**SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS**

**1.1 Spontaneous Molecular Restructuring**
Covers inpatient care if you are accidentally turned into a bowl of petunias, a 2D sketch, or a 1990s VHS tape.
Coverage up to 4 metric tons of glitter per incident.
Sub-limit: Re-solidification from a puddle state covered up to 500 liters.

**1.2 Gravity Malfunctions**
Covers medical intervention if you suddenly "fall" upwards into the sky or if gravity selectively forgets you exist.
Ceiling-walking physical therapy: Unlimited and fully covered (100%).

**1.3 Cartoon Anvil Injuries**
100% coverage for injuries sustained from pianos, anvils, safes, or oversized wooden mallets dropped from indeterminate heights.
Includes complimentary accordion-body unfolding services and tweety-bird circling removal.

**1.4 Sentient Appliance Attacks**
Covers burns, bites, or emotional trauma inflicted by a rebellious toaster, a feral blender, or an overly sarcastic smart fridge.
Coverage up to 300,000 Imaginary Dollars per appliance rebellion.

**1.5 Noodle-fication (Spaghettification)**
Complete coverage for accidental crossing of black hole event horizons. We will retrieve and reassemble your atoms.
*(Note: Reassembly has a 15% margin of error regarding the final number of limbs. Extra limbs are yours to keep).*

**1.6 Existential Dread Mitigation**
Covers up to 15 visits per timeline to a certified philosopher to convince you that you are, in fact, real and not just a simulation running on a cosmic microwave.

**1.7 Painting Entry & Exit**
Covers concussions sustained from attempting to run into painted tunnels on brick walls.
Roadrunner evasion tactics: covered twice per year.

**SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS**

**2.1 Breaking the 4th Wall**
Any injuries sustained while winking at the camera, acknowledging the narrator, or realizing you are just text in an AI prompt are strictly excluded.

**2.2 The Tuesday Paradox**
Absolutely no coverage is provided on Tuesdays. If it is Tuesday, legally, you do not exist. Any attempt to claim injury on a Tuesday will result in immediate vaporisation.

**2.3 Intentional Time Paradoxes**
Becoming your own grandfather is considered a pre-existing condition and immediately voids this policy.

**2.4 Unlicensed Cloud Yelling**
If you pull a muscle while yelling at a cloud without a proper Old Man Yells At Cloud Permit, the claim will be denied.

**2.5 Main Character Syndrome**
Treatment for dramatic slow-motion walking away from explosions without looking back is not covered, especially if shrapnel is involved.

**2.6 MacGuffin Chasing**
Injuries resulting from pursuing glowing briefcases, ancient amulets, or "the chosen one" are entirely excluded.

**SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS**

Annual Premium: 42 left socks, 3 jars of elbow grease, and one (1) reasonably good compliment.
Monthly Premium: A loud, exasperated sigh directed at the moon.
Deductible: 50 Schrute Bucks or 200 Stanley Nickels.
Co-payment: Singing the alphabet backward while hopping on one foot in front of the pharmacist.
Co-insurance: 12% of your remaining sanity after deductible is met.
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: Your absolute dignity.
Additional Fees:
Blinking too loudly fee: 5.00 Imaginary Dollars per month.

**SECTION 4: NETWORK**

This policy operates on an illogical, non-Euclidean network.
The insured may visit any doctor who operates out of a blue police box, a magical wardrobe, or a suspiciously clean swamp.
Direct billing is available if you know the secret handshake.
Out-of-network claims (visiting an actual, real-world, scientifically grounded hospital) are considered treason and will be reimbursed in Monopoly money at a rate of 0.001%.

**SECTION 5: CLAIMS**

Claims must be submitted by folding the paperwork into a paper airplane and throwing it into a mild breeze while whispering your greatest fear.
Pre-authorization is strictly required for cloning, entering parallel dimensions, or eating a slice of pizza larger than your torso.
Reimbursement is processed whenever the Raccoons feel like it, usually between 2:00 AM and 2:03 AM.
If your claim is denied, a certified mime will arrive at your residence to aggressively mock you from inside an invisible box.
