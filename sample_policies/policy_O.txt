Clipping through the floor of reality (no-clipping the Backrooms) to bypass traffic or exploit infinite resource loops immediately voids this policy and results in a permanent ban from existence.

**2.4 Apathy of the Creator**
If the universe ends simply because the Prime Architect got bored, stopped maintaining the code, and walked away, no payouts will be made, as the concept of a "payout" will have dissolved.

**2.5 Heat Death & Absolute Zero**
The inevitable, slow thermodynamic decay of all matter into a dark, frozen void is considered standard wear-and-tear of the universe and is strictly excluded.
