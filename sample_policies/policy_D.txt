HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: Apex Care Platinum Health Plan
Policy Number: ACP-2025-003
Effective Date: July 1, 2025
Insurer: Apex Health Innovations

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $1,000,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $500,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners and specialist doctors.
Coverage limit: Unlimited outpatient visits per year at $20 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ambulance services.
Emergency room: $10,000 per incident.
Ambulance: Fully covered (100%) with no incident limit.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary surgeries including pre-operative and post-operative care.
Coverage up to $500,000 per surgical event.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic, branded, and specialty drugs listed in the approved formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $15,000.
Generic drugs: 100% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: 85% reimbursement.

1.6 Mental Health Services
Covers inpatient and outpatient mental health treatment.
Unlimited outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient mental health: up to 60 days per year.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Covers prenatal visits, delivery, and postnatal care.
Normal delivery: $15,000.
Caesarean section: $25,000.
Newborn care up to 60 days post-birth.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual wellness check-up: 2 per year, fully covered (100%).
Vaccinations as recommended by WHO schedule: fully covered.
Cancer screening (mammogram, Pap smear, PSA test): covered twice per year.

1.9 Dental Care (Included)
Comprehensive dental: fillings, extractions, scaling, root canals, and crowns.
Annual dental limit: $3,000.
Orthodontics: Covered up to a lifetime maximum of $1,500.

1.10 Vision Care (Included)
Eye examination: covered twice per year.
Corrective lenses, frames, or contact lenses: $600 per year.
LASIK eye surgery: Covered up to $1,000 per eye per lifetime.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Pre-existing conditions are fully covered immediately upon the effective date
of the policy. There is no waiting period.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Any surgery or treatment primarily for aesthetic purposes is excluded,
unless determined medically necessary by a certified board of physicians.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Approved clinical trials and investigational treatments are covered
up to a lifetime limit of $50,000.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
Covered strictly for initial psychiatric evaluation and stabilization
up to 14 days of inpatient care.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Comprehensive treatment for alcohol or drug dependency is covered
up to 90 days of inpatient rehabilitation per year.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Injuries or illnesses arising directly from declared war are excluded.
Injuries from civil disturbance are covered if the insured is a non-participant.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
In vitro fertilization (IVF) and artificial insemination are covered
up to a lifetime limit of $20,000.

2.8 Alternative Medicine
Chiropractic care and acupuncture are covered up to a combined total of
40 sessions per year. Homeopathy remains excluded.

---

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $6,000.00
Monthly Premium: $500.00
Deductible: $0.00 per year
Co-payment: $20.00 per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 10% after copay
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $2,000.00 per year

Additional Fees:

* Policy administration fee: Waived
* Dental rider premium: Included in base premium
* Vision rider premium: Included in base premium

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy provides maximum benefits when using in-network providers (100%).
Out-of-network services are reimbursed at 80% of the customary and reasonable amount.
Emergency services are covered at in-network rates globally.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 180 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is required only for elective inpatient surgeries.
Reimbursement is processed within 7 business days of claim submission.
