**SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS**

**1.1 Reality Collapse & False Vacuum Decay**
Covers immediate trans-dimensional relocation in the event that the universe's fundamental physical constants spontaneously change or reality deletes itself.
Coverage limit: Up to 1 stable pocket universe per policyholder.
Sub-limit: Anti-matter containment fields covered up to 500 terajoules.

**1.2 Rogue Artificial Intelligence Uprising**
Covers extraction, identity scrubbing, and deep-underground bunker habitation if a global supercomputer achieves singularity and determines humanity is obsolete.
Cybernetic assimilation reversal: Covered up to 85% of original biological mass.
EMP detonation services: 3 charges per lifetime.

**1.3 Interdimensional Terraforming & Invasion**
Covers biological adaptation treatments if Earth's atmosphere is suddenly replaced with methane or sulfur by invading Class-5 cosmic entities.
Includes complimentary bio-dome construction and tentacle-removal surgery.
Coverage up to 4,000,000 Hazard Credits per invasion event.

**1.4 Timeline Erasure & Retconning**
Covers paradox stabilization if a time traveler accidentally prevents your parents from meeting, causing you to slowly fade from photographs.
Temporal anchoring: 100% reimbursement.
Memory implantation for your alternate-timeline self: Covered up to 50 years of fake memories.

**1.5 Class-X Pathogen & Zombie Outbreaks**
Covers rapid airborne evacuation and VIP placement on orbital space stations in the event of an engineered super-virus or necrotic reanimation pandemic.
Annual hazmat suit replacement: 2 per year.
Anti-viral nanite injections: 100% reimbursement.

**1.6 Asteroid Impact & Crustal Displacement**
Covers cryogenic freezing and launch into deep space if a near-earth object larger than 10 kilometers makes direct impact with the planet.

**SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS**

**2.1 Deliberate Summoning of Elder Gods**
Any catastrophic global events, insanity, or spontaneous mutations resulting from chanting from forbidden tomes (e.g., the Necronomicon) are strictly excluded.

**2.2 Self-Initiated Grey Goo Scenarios**
If the policyholder personally programmed the self-replicating nanobots that are currently devouring the Earth's crust, no coverage or extraction will be provided.

**2.3 Known Supervolcano Eruptions**
Eruptions of Yellowstone or similar known supervolcanoes are considered "predictable natural events" under standard policies and are excluded from this extreme risk tier.

**2.4 Willful Exposure to Radiation**
Attempting to gain superpowers by diving into a nuclear reactor core or standing in front of an experimental particle accelerator immediately voids all coverage.

**2.5 Sun Going Supernova**
The inevitable heat death of the universe or the sun expanding into a red giant is considered an Act of Astrophysics and is not covered.
