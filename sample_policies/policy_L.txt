HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

Policy Name: Aethelgard Adventurer's Health & Resurrection Pact
Policy Number: KAA-1045-DRGN
Effective Date: 1st of Sun's Height, Era of the Crown
Insurer: The Grand Clerics Guild & Iron Bank Mutual

**SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS**

**1.1 Hospitalization & Temple Care**
Covers inpatient temple stays kingdom-wide, including blessed cots, acolyte nursing services, and continuous healing chants up to 50,000 Gold Pieces (GP) per adventuring season.
Sub-limit: True Resurrection and Reincarnation covered up to 10,000 GP per year.

**1.2 Outpatient Medical Services**
Covers consultations with licensed clerics, druids, and registered apothecaries across the realm.
Coverage limit: Up to 15 healing sessions or herbalist visits per year.
Telepathic Consultations (Sending Spell): Unlimited and fully covered (100%).

**1.3 Emergency Services**
Covers battlefield triage and griffin/wyvern evacuation services.
Emergency Temple Admittance: 0 GP if admitted, 50 GP if discharged with minor wounds.
Teleportation Evacuation: Fully covered up to 5,000 GP per dungeon dive.

**1.4 Magical & Surgical Procedures**
Covers medically necessary surgeries and high-level abjurations, including curse removal, magical appendage reattachment, and petrification reversal.
Coverage up to 15,000 GP per event.

**1.5 Prescription Potions and Salves**
Covers standardized alchemy and potion brewing by a Guild-certified alchemist.
Annual potion benefit limit: 2,000 GP.
Minor Healing Potions: 100% reimbursement.
Supreme Healing and Elixirs of Life (Branded): 75% reimbursement.

**1.6 Mental Health Services**
Covers treatment for magical madness, psychic damage, and eldritch horrors exposure.
Up to 10 Bardic Inspiration or mind-soothing spells per year.
Inpatient sanctuary rest: up to 20 days per year.

**1.7 Familiar and Mount Care**
Subject to a 3-moon waiting period from the effective date.
Standard horse or hound revival: 500 GP.
Exotic mounts (Hippogriffs, Dire Wolves): 1,500 GP.
Familiar recasting materials covered up to 100 GP per year.

**1.8 Preventive Care**
Annual divine blessing and aura cleansing: 1 per year, fully covered (100%).
Warding spells, including travel wards (e.g., Protection from Evil, Endure Elements): fully covered.
Alignment shift screening: covered once per year.

**1.9 Dental & Tusk Care (Half-Orc & Barbarian Rider)**
Basic dental: wooden teeth, gold fillings, tusk sharpening.
Annual dental limit: 300 GP.
Magical jaw replacement not covered.

**1.10 Vision Care**
Annual magical eye examination (Detect Magic / True Seeing): covered once per year.
Eyepatches, monocles of comprehending languages, or glass eyes: 150 GP per year.

**SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS**

**2.1 Pre-existing Curses**
Any hex, blood curse, or demonic pact established within 12 moons before the policy start date is not covered during the first year, unless transferring from a rival deity’s health plan.

**2.2 Lycanthropy and Vampirism**
Treatment for, or resulting from, contracting Lycanthropy or Vampirism is strictly excluded unless the "Creature of the Night" Premium Rider is purchased.

**2.3 Necromancy**
Treatments utilizing unholy magic, raising of the undead, or consultations with liches are entirely excluded and will result in immediate policy termination and a bounty.

**2.4 Foolish Dungeon Delving**
Injuries resulting from actively ignoring warning signs, stepping on obvious pressure plates, or splitting the party are excluded.

**2.5 Dragon Breath Weapons**
Treatment for burns, acid melting, or freezing caused by ancient or adult dragons is not covered if the insured initiated combat.

**2.6 Acts of Vengeful Gods**
Smiting, polymorphing into a newt, or being banished to another plane of existence by a deity is considered an Act of God and is excluded.

**2.7 Deck of Many Things**
Any negative health effects, soul imprisonment, or loss of wealth drawn from a Deck of Many Things are entirely excluded.

**SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS**

Annual Premium: 1,200.00 Gold Pieces (GP)
Monthly Premium: 100.00 GP
Deductible: 50.00 GP per year
Co-payment: 5.00 Silver Pieces (SP) per apothecary visit
Co-insurance: 10% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: 500.00 GP per year
Additional Fees:
Scroll administration fee: 2.00 GP
Dungeon Hazard Rider: 15.00 GP per month

**SECTION 4: NETWORK**

This policy operates on an open-realm network within allied territories.
The insured may visit any Temple of the Light, registered Druid Grove, or Guild Apothecary.
Direct billing is available within the High Cleric Provider Network.
Out-of-network claims (Hedge Witches, Swamp Hags, Unlicensed Bards) are reimbursed at 60% of the customary and reasonable amount, subject to a detect poison scan.

**SECTION 5: CLAIMS**

Claims must be submitted via parchment, messenger raven, or sending stone within 90 days of the date of injury.
Pre-authorization from a High Priest is strictly required for resurrections, astral projection recovery, and limb regeneration.
Reimbursement is processed in the policyholder's chosen currency (Gold, Silver, or Copper pieces) within 10 dawns.
