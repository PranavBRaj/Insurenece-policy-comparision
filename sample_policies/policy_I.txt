HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Name: MediCore Senior Advantage Health Plan
Policy Number: MSA-2027-008
Effective Date: April 1, 2027
Insurer: MediCore Senior Services

---

SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS

1.1 Hospitalization
Covers inpatient hospital stays including room and board, nursing services,
and general nursing care up to $750,000 per policy year.
Sub-limit: ICU/Critical Care covered up to $250,000 per year.

1.2 Outpatient Medical Services
Covers consultations with general practitioners, geriatricians, and specialist doctors.
Coverage limit: Up to 50 outpatient visits per year at $15 per visit.

1.3 Emergency Services
Covers emergency room visits and ambulance services.
Emergency room: $1,500 per incident.
Ambulance: Fully covered (100%) up to 5 incidents per year.

1.4 Surgical Procedures
Covers medically necessary surgeries including joint replacements and cardiac bypass.
Coverage up to $300,000 per surgical event.

1.5 Prescription Drugs
Covers generic, branded, and chronic condition maintenance drugs listed in the approved formulary.
Annual drug benefit limit: $25,000.
Generic drugs: 100% reimbursement.
Branded drugs: 80% reimbursement.

1.6 Mental Health & Cognitive Services
Covers inpatient and outpatient mental health treatment, including Alzheimer's and dementia care.
Up to 30 outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient cognitive care: up to 45 days per year.

1.7 Maternity and Newborn Care
Not covered under this plan.

1.8 Preventive Care
Annual senior wellness check-up: 2 per year, fully covered (100%).
Vaccinations (e.g., Shingles, Pneumococcal, Influenza): fully covered.
Cancer screening (mammogram, Pap smear, PSA test, colonoscopy): covered annually.

1.9 Dental Care (Included)
Comprehensive dental including dentures, crowns, and implants.
Annual dental limit: $5,000.
Orthodontics not covered.

1.10 Vision & Hearing Care (Included)
Annual eye and hearing examinations: covered once per year.
Corrective lenses or frames: $400 per year.
Hearing aids: Covered up to $2,000 per ear every 3 years.

---

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS

2.1 Pre-existing Conditions
Pre-existing conditions are covered after a 3-month waiting period from the policy start date.

2.2 Cosmetic Procedures
Any surgery or treatment primarily for aesthetic purposes is excluded.

2.3 Experimental Treatments
Treatments not approved by the relevant health authority or classified as
investigational or experimental are not covered.

2.4 Self-inflicted Injuries
Covered only for initial stabilization and psychiatric evaluation up to 7 days.

2.5 Substance Abuse
Treatment for alcohol or drug dependency is covered up to 14 days of inpatient
rehabilitation per year.

2.6 War and Civil Unrest
Injuries or illnesses arising directly from war, invasion, or civil
disturbance are excluded from all coverage.

2.7 Fertility Treatments
Not covered under this plan.

2.8 Alternative Medicine
Acupuncture and chiropractic care are covered up to a combined total of
20 sessions per year for pain management only.

---

SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS

Annual Premium: $8,400.00
Monthly Premium: $700.00
Deductible: $500.00 per year
Co-payment: $15.00 per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 15% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $4,500.00 per year

Additional Fees:

* Policy administration fee: Waived
* Dental rider premium: Included
* Vision/Hearing rider premium: Included

---

SECTION 4: NETWORK

This policy provides full benefits when using the MediCore Senior Advantage network.
Out-of-network services are reimbursed at 60% of the Medicare-approved amount.
Emergency services are covered at in-network rates regardless of provider.

---

SECTION 5: CLAIMS

Claims must be submitted within 180 days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is required for joint replacements, advanced imaging, and cognitive care admissions.
Reimbursement is processed within 10 business days of claim approval.
