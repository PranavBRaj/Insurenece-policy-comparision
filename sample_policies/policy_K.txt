HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

Policy Name: Galactic Federation Health & Respawn Plan
Policy Number: GF-3027-X99
Effective Date: Stardate 4529.1
Insurer: OmniCorp Interstellar Assurance

**SECTION 1: COVERAGE BENEFITS**

**1.1 Hospitalization**
Covers inpatient medical bay stays sector-wide, including anti-gravity room and board, automated android nursing services, and full bacta tank immersion up to 50,000,000 Galactic Credits per standard solar year.
Sub-limit: Cloning and consciousness transfer covered up to 10,000,000 Credits per year.

**1.2 Outpatient Medical Services**
Covers consultations with biological, cybernetic, and holographic specialists across all known galaxies.
Coverage limit: Up to 42 physical outpatient visits per year.
Telepathic and Holographic consultations: Unlimited and fully covered (100%).

**1.3 Emergency Services**
Covers emergency teleportation and warp-speed ambulance services universally.
Emergency medical bay: 0 Credits if admitted, 500 Credits if discharged without cybernetic enhancement.
Wormhole Evacuation & Repatriation: Fully covered up to 1,000,000,000 Credits per incident.

**1.4 Surgical Procedures**
Covers medically necessary biological and cybernetic surgeries, including pre-operative scanning and post-operative recalibration.
Coverage up to 3,000,000 Credits per surgical event.

**1.5 Prescription Drugs**
Covers generic chemical, nanobot, and alchemy-based prescriptions universally.
Annual drug benefit limit: 50,000 Credits.
Nanobot injections: 100% reimbursement.
Mana and Health Potions (Branded): 80% reimbursement.

**1.6 Mental Health Services**
Covers biological and synthetic mental health treatment.
Up to 20 dream-walking or neural-linking outpatient therapy sessions per year.
Inpatient memory wiping/restructuring: up to 30 days per year.

**1.7 Maternity, Hatching, and Spawning Care**
Subject to a 3-cycle waiting period from the effective date.
Standard mammalian delivery: 100,000 Credits.
Egg incubation and hatching support: 150,000 Credits.
Larval stage care up to 45 orbital days post-emergence.

**1.8 Preventive Care**
Annual molecular wellness scan: 1 per year, fully covered (100%).
Immunizations, including interplanetary travel inoculations (e.g., Space Madness, Martian Flu): fully covered.
Genetic mutation screening: covered twice per year.

**1.9 Dental & Mandible Care (Predator & Herbivore Rider)**
Basic dental: laser fillings, fang extractions, beak scaling.
Annual dental limit: 12,000 Credits.
Tusk and horn realignment: covered up to 5,000 Credits lifetime limit.

**1.10 Vision and Sensor Care**
Annual optical and thermal sensor examination: covered once per year.
Corrective lenses, monocles, or cyber-optic upgrades: 3,000 Credits per year.

**SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS AND EXCEPTIONS**

**2.1 Pre-existing Conditions**
Any curse, hex, or genetic anomaly diagnosed or treated within 2 light-years before the policy start date is not covered during the first orbit.

**2.2 Cosmetic Procedures**
Any surgery primarily for aesthetic purposes (e.g., adding unnecessary tentacles, glitter-infused scales) is excluded.

**2.3 Experimental Treatments**
Treatments not approved by the Intergalactic Medical Council or the High Wizarding Board are excluded.

**2.4 Spontaneous Combustion**
Any injuries resulting from unexplained spontaneous combustion due to faulty teleportation are entirely excluded.

**2.5 Zombification and Undeath**
Treatment for becoming a member of the undead is not covered. Reanimation is strictly prohibited under this policy.

**2.6 Temporal Paradoxes**
Injuries sustained from meeting your past or future self are not covered. Excluded if the insured travels to a timeline where a "Do Not Exist" advisory is in effect.

**2.7 Alien Abduction**
Injuries or memory loss resulting from unsanctioned alien abduction are excluded unless the Extraterrestrial Encounter Rider is purchased.

**2.8 Extreme Physics Sports**
Injuries from hazardous activities (e.g., black hole surfing, unshielded solar diving, competitive meteor riding) are excluded.

**SECTION 3: PREMIUMS AND COSTS**

Annual Premium: 54,000.00 Galactic Credits (or 3 vials of unicorn tears)
Monthly Premium: 4,500.00 Galactic Credits
Deductible: 5,000.00 Credits per year
Co-payment: 200.00 Credits per outpatient visit
Co-insurance: 15% after deductible is met
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: 50,000.00 Credits per year
Additional Fees:
Policy administration fee: 250.00 Credits
Time Travel Insurance Rider: 600.00 Credits per month

**SECTION 4: NETWORK**

This policy operates on a pan-dimensional open-access network.
The insured may visit any licensed biological practitioner, AI doctor, or Shaman within the Milky Way and Andromeda galaxies.
Direct billing is available within the OmniCorp Prime Network.
Out-of-network claims (outside the current dimension) are reimbursed at 50% of the customary and reasonable amount for that specific reality.

**SECTION 5: CLAIMS**

Claims must be submitted via telepathy, faster-than-light subspace transmission, or carrier pigeon within 90 solar days of the date of service.
Pre-authorization is strictly required for timeline alterations, cybernetic brain swaps, and non-emergency cryogenic freezing.
Reimbursement is processed in the policyholder's chosen currency (Credits, Gold Doubloons, or Antimatter) within 7 planetary rotations.
